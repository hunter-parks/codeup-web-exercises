FAQs About National Parks

Who is the director of the National Park Service
Shawn Benge
How many employees are in the National Park Service
Permanent, temporary, and seasonal employees: Approximately 20,000
How many areas are in the National Park system
The system includes 423 areas
Can I bring a pet to a national park
Some national parks welcome pets, in developed areas, on many trails and campgrounds
How do I make reservations for camping/lodging in national parks
For campground reservations you can visit the website Recreations.gov. Not all parks partake in this service, many are first come first serve basis
